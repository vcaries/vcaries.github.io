**Years:** 2022 – 2025

**Duration:** 24 hours / year

**Location:** [École Centrale de Lyon](https://www.ec-lyon.fr/en/)

**Course:** External Aerodynamics

**Level:** MSc, final year

**Description:** Wind-tunnel lab sessions on aerodynamic force measurement. Students design and run experiments, analyze the results, and compare them with RANS (Reynolds-Averaged Navier-Stokes) simulations. Taught in English.
